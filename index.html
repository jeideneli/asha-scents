Our Story

Founded in Paris in 2010, LuxeScents was born from a passion for capturing fleeting moments in bottle. Our master perfumer, Antoine Lacroix, creates each fragrance using only the finest ingredients sourced from around the world.

We believe perfume is an invisible accessory that completes your personal style. Each LuxeScents fragrance is meticulously crafted to tell a story and evoke emotion, blending tradition with modern sophistication.
